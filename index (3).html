Why GoviyaMart

Three Pillars of Direct Trade

Everything we do is designed to create a more honest, fair, and prosperous agricultural ecosystem in Sri Lanka.

🤝

100% Direct Trade

Every transaction goes straight from farmer to buyer. Zero commission agents, zero delay, zero markup. The price you agree on is the price you pay — nothing more.

⚖️

Fair Pricing for Farmers

Farmers set their own market-rate prices. Live price intelligence shows current wholesale rates so rural growers always know their harvest's true worth.

🏅

Fresh & Certified Quality

Every listing carries harvest-date freshness data and government-verified organic certification where applicable. What you see is what arrives at your door.
